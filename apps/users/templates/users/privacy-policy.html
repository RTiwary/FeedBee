Privacy Policy

This policy describes how we use your data.

Your privacy is important to us. It is Classbop's policy to respect your privacy regarding any information we may collect from you across our website, http://www.classbop.com, and other sites we own and operate.

Our website may link to external sites that are not operated by us. Please be aware that we have no control over the content and practices of these sites, and cannot accept responsibility or liability for their respective privacy policies.

You are free to refuse our request for your personal information, with the understanding that we may be unable to provide you with some of your desired services.

Your continued use of our website will be regarded as acceptance of our practices around privacy and personal information. If you have any questions about how we handle user data and personal information, feel free to contact us.


1. Information We Collect

We only ask for personal information when we truly need it to provide a service to you. We collect it by fair and lawful means, with your knowledge and consent. We also let you know why we’re collecting it and how it will be used.

We collect:

• Your first name, last name, username, password, email address, timezone, and whether you are a teacher or student when you register for an account

• User content (e.g., survey questions, ) that you post to Classbop

• Feedback that you provide through our feedback tab


2. Data Collection Methods

We collect data from you and from third parties that you log into Classbop with.

We only retain collected information for as long as necessary to provide you with your requested service. What data we store, we’ll protect within commercially acceptable means to prevent loss and theft, as well as unauthorized access, disclosure, copying, use or modification.

We collect data through:

• Our platform: data is collected anywhere on our platform where content can be posted to our service

• Google Sign In: We collect your first name, last name, and email address when you log in to Classbop with Google Sign In. We only use this data to create an account in our platform and the data will ultimately be stored in our database as registration information. The data that we collect from your Google Sign In will not be shared.

• Microsoft Sign In: We collect your first name, last name, and email address when you log in to Classbop with Microsoft Sign In. We only use this data to create an account in our platform and the data will ultimately be stored in our database as registration information. The data that we collect from your Microsoft Sign In will not be shared.


3. Data Sharing

We don’t share any personally identifying information publicly or with third-parties, except when required to by law.

This policy is effective as of 20 August 2020.
